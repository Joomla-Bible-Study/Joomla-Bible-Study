Comments Help Screen

Users can now add comments to the studies on the front end. You will need to turn on this function, which is found in the component parameters. You can also determine who can enter comments and whether the comments need to be approved on the backend by an admin. This listing is the comments that have been submitted. Simply click on the link to view the comment, or simply click on the red x to publish or the green check to unpublish.
